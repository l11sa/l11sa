An easy-to-make homemade apple pie with lots of apples.

Ingridient Amount Grams
Eggs 5 -
Sugar 2.4 dl 165 gr
Oil 1.6 dl 105 gr
Wheat flour 5 dl 350 gr
Baking powder 1.5 teaspoons -
Peeled apple pieces - 400-600 gr
Cinnamon 1 teaspoon -
Salt a pinch of -

Instructions:

  • Beat eggs with sugar, add oil.
  • In another bowl, mix the flour with salt and baking powder and add the flour mixture to the egg-oil mixture.
  • Mix and add the apple pieces.
  • Put the dough in a 20-cm springform cake tin lined with baking paper.
  • Mix and add the apple pieces.
  • If you wish, sprinkle with cinnamon.
  • Bake in a 190-degree oven for approx. 35-45 minutes.
